Pasta Recipe

This delicious pesto pasta will have you begging your girl to learn the recipe!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Pesto
  • Basil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook onions and basil in oil
  3. Mix in pesto and other desired seasonings
  4. Grate cheese on top and serve!
