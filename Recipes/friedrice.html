Fried Rice Recipe

A quick and easy dish that can taste great without much effort!

Ingredients

  • Cooked White Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Frozen Vegetables
  • Eggs
  1. Add rice to wok and cook until toasted
  2. Make space in middle of wok and add in vegetables
  3. Push rice to side and add in egg
  4. Add in soy sauce and mix all ingredients together
  5. Serve!
