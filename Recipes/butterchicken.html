Butter Chicken Recipe

Learn this recipe so you can finally pretend you're cultured around your friends!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Thighs or Breasts
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Minced Garlic
  • Minced Ginger
  • Garam Masala
  • Turmeric
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  1. Combine chicken with all other ingredients and marinate
  2. Heat oil in large pot and add chicken pieces in batches until browned
  3. Heat butter in same pan and fry up onions
  4. Add ginger and garlic and saute
  5. Add crushed tomatoes and seasoning and blend until smooth and thick
  6. Combine chicken back in and mix
